About Timmy Bolivia

"The loss of a pet was a big problem for owners. We use posters that are taken down after a week. We use our Facebook account to publish a lost pet search ad, but this ad only reaches your contacts. I had these problems when my family search my dog "Timmy", and we wondered how to reach more people. What about a web page with a page where users can upload information about their lost pet, and users can report found pets. A place where people who love animals can report and help owners who have lost their pets."

Do you want to know more about us?

Our Mision:

To reunite lost dogs with their families through community support, technology, and fast information sharing

Our Vision:

Every lost dog safely back home.

Our Values:

We believe in the power of people helping people.
We value clear, reliable, and useful information.
Time matters. We act quickly to increase the chances of reunion.
